Lise Meitner: The Mother of the Atom Bomb

This documentary highlights Austrian physicist Lise Meitner, long erased from the history of nuclear fission.

Through testimonies and archival images, the film restores her central role and her scientific integrity.

Duration: 52 min • Directors: Wolf von Truchsess, Andreas G Wagner
